About GLUG-PESCE

GNU/LINUX Users have formed a group called GLUG in P.E.S. College of Engineering, Mandya and this group is strongly supported by Free Software Movement of Karnataka (FSMK) group. The main objective of this group is to provide awareness about free software to students. GLUG, PESCE is active in conducting technical events like regular workshops, Hands-on sessions, conferences etc. GLUG, PESCE is co-ordinated by Dr. Minavathi, Professor and HOD, IS&E Department, PESCE, Mandya. The main objective of this group is to provide awareness of free software in students. Free software means software that respects user's freedom and community. Roughly, it means that the users have the freedom to run, copy, distribute, study, change and improve the software. Thus, "free software" is a matter of liberty, not price. GLUG campaign for these freedoms because everyone deserves them. With these freedoms, the users (both individually and collectively) control the program and what it does for them.
